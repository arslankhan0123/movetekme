Our Services

Bismillah Engineering is a provider of integrated solutions across multiple industries, with a legacy of over four decades. We have established our reputation as a manufacturer, assembler, and fabrication service provider for Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Automotive, Construction, Pharmaceutical, Textile, and Energy sectors, committed to end-to-end manufacturing and fabrication services.

Industrial Sheds
  • Design and construction of robust industrial sheds tailored to client needs.
  • Incorporation of quality materials ensuring structural integrity.
  • Services include Portal Frame sheds, prefabricated Industrial sheds, Fiber Reinforced Plastic sheds, warehouse Storage Sheds, Cold Storage sheds, Agricultural and hybrid industrial sheds.
Panel Fabrication
  • Custom fabrication of switchboard panels to meet project-specific requirements.
  • Utilization of advanced techniques for precision and durability.
  • Services include all types of panel structures Including HV, MV, and LV structures.
Crane Structures
  • Fabrication and installation of crane structures for efficient material handling.
  • Compliance with safety standards and load-bearing specifications.
  • Services include Portal Cranes, deck Cranes, Agricultural Cranes, Mobile Cranes, and Tower cranes.
Racks and Grills
  • Custom racks and grills fabrication for storage and security applications.
  • Versatile designs to optimize space utilization.
  • Services include Pallet Racks, flow Racks, Selective Racks, Shelving system, Mezzanine Racks, Decorative metal Grills, Ventilation Racks, Security Grills, Automotive Grills, Drainage and Walkway Grills.
Doors, Windows, and Gates
  • Fabrication of durable and secure doors, windows, and gates.
  • Custom designs to match architectural and security requirements.
  • Services include Steel doors, aluminum Doors, Sliding Doors, Casement Windows, Bay windows, Automatic Gates, Sliding gates, laser cutting design Gates, and Mesh Gates Designs cutting on Laser machine.
Solar Structures
  • Fabrication and installation of solar support structures, including L1 and L2 types.
  • Structural design to withstand environmental factors and ensure solar panel stability.
  • Services include Ground Mounted Solar Structure, Solar carport Structures, Solar Rooftop structures, Floating Solar Structures, Rotating Solar Structures, Solar canopies, Solar Trackers, and On/Off-Grid structures.
Facilities And Activities
  • Modern manufacturing units for precision-engineered products (High, Medium, Low voltage panels, Distribution Boxes).
  • Research and development centers for innovation and continuous improvement, especially for imported product replacement.
  • Focus on Green Energy solutions (Solar) for industries and agriculture.
  • Quality control labs and equipment ensuring international standards.
  • Dedicated areas for fabrication, machining, and die casting.
  • Warehousing facilities for efficient inventory management.
  • Modern machinery including CNC-FIBER LASER, CNC MILLING, CNC BENDER, CNC SHEARING, and CNC LATHE.

Market Presence
